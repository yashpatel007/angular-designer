Unix Time {{currentDate.getTime()}}

Unix Time also known as Epoch time is a system for describing a point in time. It is the number of seconds that have elapsed since the Unix epoch, minus leap seconds; the Unix epoch is 00:00:00 UTC on 1 January 1970 (an arbitrary date).
